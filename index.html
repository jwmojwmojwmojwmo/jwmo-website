About Me

Currently, I'm a Bachelor's of Science student at UBC, pursuing a Major in Computer Science, Minor in Data Science, and a Master's of Management through UBC's B+MM Dual Degree program.

I spend most of my time building things that probably shouldn't exist. For example, I've been working on SupremeASM (my own custom assembly language), a Jeff the Landshark Discord bot, and trying to figure out how to make cool websites. Although I love coding, HTML & CSS simply does not speak to me sometimes. I am not the graphics design man we need in society. Most of the time I enjoy backend more, but that doesn't mean I can't make websites!
